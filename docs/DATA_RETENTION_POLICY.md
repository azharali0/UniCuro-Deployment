# Data Retention Policy

## Example Retention Rules

- Account profile: 6 years after closure where contract/legal basis applies.
- Payments/wallet: 7 years for accounting/legal records.
- Security logs: 24 months.
- Marketing consent: until withdrawn, plus audit evidence period.
- Wellbeing check-ins: 12 months, unless law or safety obligation requires otherwise.
- Academic generated assets: configurable by student/university policy.
- Support tickets: 3 years unless legal escalation.

## Production requirements

- automated retention jobs
- legal hold override
- deletion evidence
- anonymisation for analytics
- institution reporting aggregation
